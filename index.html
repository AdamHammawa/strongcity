About our Company

Get to know us, when we started, what we offer, how we do it.

Strong City Global Investment Limited is a company that offers premium services in General Contractor Services, Buildings, Real Estate, Supply and Marketing of varios categories of Products in Nigeria. The company was founded and Incoporated in 2012. It is owned and managed by professional individuals with the aim of attainig perfection and customer satisfaction.
Strong City has its HeadOffice located in No 1 Chiroma Street off Lamido Abba Road, Yola , Adamawa state. It's Branch office is located at No 7 Darazo Road, Federal low cost, Bauchi State.
