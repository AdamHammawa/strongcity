About our Company

Get to know us, when we started, what we offer, how we do it.

Strong City Global Investment Limited is a company that offers premium services in different aspects of Buildings, Real Estate, Supply and Marketing of varios categories of Products in Nigeria. The company was founded in 2010. It is owned and managed by professional individuals with the aim of attainig perfection and customer satisfaction.
